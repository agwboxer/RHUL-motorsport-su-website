WELCOME TO ROYAL HOLLOWAYS MOTORSPORT CLUB

Are you a motorsport enthusiast? Do you have a need for speed? Look no further – Royal Holloways Motorsport Club is your gateway to the thrilling world of motorsports on campus.

Our social media

The best way to contact us is via DM on Instagram or by sending a message in our discord

What we offer

    • Race Watches: We host watch parties for major races, grab some popcorn and enjoy a free viewing of your favourite races live.
    • Go-Karting: Race in a high performance go-kart and compete against your peers for bragging rights. We welcome all skill levels to introduce everyone to the competitive side of motorsport.
    • Sim Racing: Similarly, sim racing allows you to compete against your friends in a virtual race. Drive your favourite cars on tracks from all around the world.
    • BUKC: Drive the fastest rental karts in the UK against other universities in the British Universities Karting Championship.
    • BUSR: Compete in virtual races against other universities in EA F1, Assetto Corsa Competizione, iRacing and EA WRC.

Getting Involved

We welcome everyone of all skill levels to come and try their hand at motorsports. Our membership is £[TBD] for the entire year! This will give you access to all our sim and kart races we organise. We also host regular F1 watchalongs every race weekend and any other races you might want to watch! Please don't be afraid to send is a DM or ask a question on our discord our committee are more than happy to respond!
